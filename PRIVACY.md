# Privacy Notice

This app stores personal portfolio data including holdings and transactions **locally on your own infrastructure**.

- No data is transmitted to any server by the authors of this project.
- When you configure and use external AI services (e.g. Anthropic API for Research Chat), your data is sent directly from your instance to that provider and is subject to their respective privacy policy and terms of service.
- You are solely responsible for the security of your deployment, your data, and any API keys you configure.
- Do not store sensitive personal identifiers such as tax IDs or bank account numbers in this app.

See [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy) if you use the Research Chat feature.
